What is it?

You can feed the less fortunate in New Zealand and help stomp out hunger in one easy step plus you get a one-of-a-kind ceramic bowl! With just a $20 donation you will become part of an international movement.

How can I donate?

You can support from the 1st of August through our generous supporters, Wellington Potters Association , the Hill Street Market or from the DCM itself over the month of August. All funds go directly to Downtown Committee Ministry (DCM).
